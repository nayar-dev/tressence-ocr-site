Privacy Policy

Last updated:

This Privacy Policy explains how Nayar Mart (“we”, “our”, or “us”) collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application and related services (together, the “App” or “Service”).

By using Nayar Mart, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with this Policy, please do not use the App.

1. About Nayar Mart

Nayar Mart is a marketplace for buying and selling brand-new and second-hand items, mainly designed for direct-to-consumer (D2C) use. Users can browse products, post listings, communicate with other users, and complete transactions outside or inside the App depending on features we provide.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information when you use Nayar Mart:

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Account information: such as your name, email address, phone number, profile photo, and password (or authentication information from third-party sign-in providers).
  • Profile and listing information: product titles, descriptions, images, prices, category, condition (new/used), and other details you choose to share.
  • Communications: messages you send within the App (if messaging is enabled), and any information you provide when you contact our support team.

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device and log data: IP address, device model, operating system version, app version, language, and usage logs (such as pages/screens viewed and actions taken in the App).
  • Usage information: interactions with listings, searches, favorites, and other in-app behavior used to improve your experience and the Service.

2.3 Location Information (If Enabled)

With your permission, we may collect approximate or precise location information to help show you relevant listings near you or to improve search and filtering features. You can control location access in your device settings.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for purposes such as:

  • Creating and managing your Nayar Mart account.
  • Displaying your listings and profile to other users.
  • Enabling communication between buyers and sellers (where supported).
  • Improving and personalizing the App and user experience.
  • Monitoring usage, troubleshooting, and protecting against fraud or abuse.
  • Sending you service-related notifications (for example, important updates or security alerts).
  • Complying with legal obligations and enforcing our terms and policies.

4. Legal Basis (If Applicable)

If you are in a region where a legal basis is required (such as the European Economic Area), we rely on one or more of the following:

  • Performance of a contract – to provide you with the Service.
  • Legitimate interests – to maintain and improve the App, prevent abuse, and protect our rights.
  • Consent – for certain optional features (for example, notifications or location services).
  • Legal obligations – to comply with applicable laws and regulations.

5. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information in the following cases:

  • With other users: certain information such as your name, profile image, and listing details may be visible to other users as part of normal marketplace functionality.
  • Service providers: we may share data with third parties who help us operate and improve the App (such as hosting, analytics, or messaging providers) under appropriate confidentiality and security obligations.
  • Legal and safety: we may disclose information if required by law or if we believe it is reasonably necessary to protect the rights, property, or safety of our users, the public, or us.
  • Business transfers: if we are involved in a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction, subject to continued protection.

6. Third-Party Services

The App may use third-party services such as analytics providers, cloud hosting, push notification services, authentication providers, or payment services. These services may collect information in accordance with their own privacy policies.

We recommend you review the privacy policies of any third-party services you interact with through the App.

7. Data Retention

We keep your information for as long as necessary to provide the Service, fulfill the purposes set out in this Privacy Policy, and comply with applicable laws. When information is no longer needed, we will delete or anonymize it.

8. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have rights such as:

  • Accessing the personal information we hold about you.
  • Correcting inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Requesting deletion of your account and associated personal data (subject to legal retention requirements).
  • Objecting to or restricting certain types of processing.
  • Withdrawing consent where processing is based on consent.

You can manage certain settings directly in the App (for example, editing your profile or deleting listings). For other requests, please contact us using the details in the “Contact Us” section below.

9. Children’s Privacy

Nayar Mart is not intended for children under the age of 13 (or the minimum age required in your region). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe that a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will take steps to remove the information.

10. Data Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or alteration. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

11. International Transfers

Your information may be stored and processed on servers located in different countries. By using the App, you consent to the transfer of your information to countries that may have different data protection laws than your country of residence, where permitted by law.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the “Last updated” date at the top of the page. We encourage you to review this Policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your information.
