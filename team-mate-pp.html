Privacy Policy – Team Mate by Nayar

Last Updated: February 20, 2026

Team Mate by Nayar is a workplace management platform designed for employee attendance, payroll, and HR operations. We are committed to protecting employee and organizational data with transparency, security, and compliance.

Our Commitment: We never sell, rent, or commercially monetize employee personal data. All information is used strictly for HR, payroll, and workplace operational purposes.

1. Scope of This Policy

This policy applies to employees, contractors, and authorized users who access the Team Mate HR platform via mobile apps, web dashboards, or integrations.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Account & Identity Information

  • Professional email address
  • Employee ID / staff code
  • Name, department, and employment profile details

2.2 Attendance & Work Activity Data

  • Clock-in and clock-out timestamps
  • Shift assignments and attendance records
  • Leave applications and approvals

2.3 Location Data (for Attendance Verification)

  • Approximate or precise location during attendance submission
  • Used solely to verify presence at authorized workplaces
  • Not used for continuous tracking or monitoring

2.4 Payroll & HR Data

  • Salary components and adjustments
  • Allowances, incentives, and deductions
  • Attendance-based payroll calculations

3. How We Use Information

Collected data is used strictly for operational HR functions:

  • Attendance and time tracking
  • Payroll calculation and payslip generation
  • HR reporting and compliance
  • Workforce planning and internal management
  • Security and fraud prevention

4. Data Ownership

All employee data belongs to the organization (employer). Team Mate acts only as a data processor providing HR software infrastructure.

5. Data Sharing

We do not share employee data with third parties except:

  • When required by law or regulatory authority
  • When authorized by the employer for payroll processing
  • For infrastructure hosting and security services under strict confidentiality agreements

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard safeguards including:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS)
  • Secure access control and authentication
  • Role-based permission systems
  • Secure cloud storage

7. Data Retention

Data is stored only as long as required by the employer’s HR policy, payroll regulations, and compliance requirements.

8. Employee Rights

Employees may request through their employer:

  • Access to personal HR records
  • Correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion where legally permissible

9. Children's Privacy

Team Mate by Nayar is designed for workplace use and is not intended for individuals under 18 years old.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Updates will be published within the application and on official communication channels.
